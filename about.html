About

José Neira received his BFA from SUNY College at Purchase, NY, in 1984, where he was awarded The Dean’s Commendation for Excellence in Design. Following he received his MFA from the School of Art at Yale —where he had the privilege of learning directly from Paul Rand, Armin Hofmann, and Shigeo Fukuda, among others giants.

In 1991 José Neira returned to Santiago. Since then, he has been working in the arts and culture sector, and also in the private sector, through branding, packaging, editorial, signage and museography projects. In parallel he has been involved in higher education, as adjunct faculty at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile, Universidad de Chile, Universidad Finis Terrae, and Universidad Andrés Bello —he was appointed Director of the Design School in the later, between 2007 and 2009.
